How we work

A simple, four-stage path from your first inquiry to gummies on your shelves.

01
Discovery & NDA

You share your concept, target market, claims and budget. We sign a mutual NDA so your IP and formula concepts stay protected from day one.

02
Formulation & Sampling

Our food chemists develop your gummy to the requested taste, texture, potency and compliance spec. You evaluate samples, we revise until you sign off.

03
Plant Trial & Production

A plant trial validates the formula at scale. Full production follows in our FDA-registered, cGMP facility with multi-stage QC testing at every step.

04
Packaging & Delivery

Bottle, pouch, jar or bulk. Labels applied, induction-sealed, retail-ready. Full Certificate of Analysis ships with every batch.
