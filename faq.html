Getting started & process

Inquiries, quotes, and how a project unfolds.

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?+

We keep MOQs low to support emerging brands. Exact minimums depend on formula complexity, ingredients and packaging format — reach out with your specs and we will confirm the number that fits your launch.

How long does it take from concept to finished product?+

Typical timelines run a few weeks for private-label orders using proven formulas, and several weeks for fully custom formulations. Our vertically integrated R&D and production team compresses what usually takes nine months at legacy co-mans into a matter of weeks.

Do you offer samples?+

Yes. After we align on the formulation spec, our team produces physical samples for you to evaluate taste, texture, color and overall feel. We revise until you sign off — only then do we move to full production.

Do I need to have a formula ready?+

No. You can bring your existing formula for us to scale (contract manufacturing), collaborate with our R&D team on a fully custom formulation, or choose from our library of proven gummy recipes for private label. Whatever stage you are at, we can start there.

Do you sign NDAs?+

Absolutely. After your initial inquiry, we sign a mutual NDA before any technical detail is exchanged. Your formula concepts, active-ingredient profile, label copy and business plans stay protected from day one.

What is the quote process?+

Standard private-label formulas are quoted immediately. Custom formulations typically receive pricing within 24 to 48 hours after our formulation team reviews the spec. Quotes include a full breakdown — no hidden fees, no surprise charges.

Can you work with my existing formula?+

Yes. We regularly take over formulas from clients whose previous manufacturers could not hit potency or deliver on time. We will review the spec, flag any compliance issues, and run a plant trial before committing to full production.

What are your payment terms?+

Production orders typically require a 50% deposit at PO placement, with the balance due before raw material procurement begins. We finalize exact terms in your quote so you always know what is due and when.

How many revisions do I get on a custom formula?+

We revise until you sign off. There is no arbitrary revision limit — our R&D team keeps working on taste, texture, color, potency and shape until the sample matches your brief.

Do you handle packaging and labeling?+

Yes. We offer bottles, stand-up pouches, jars, sachets and bulk bags. In-line filling, induction sealing, tamper-evident banding and custom label application are all done in-house, so your finished product ships retail-ready.

Do you ship internationally?+

We ship domestically within the US as standard. International freight can be arranged, with the client handling the destination-country import and regulatory requirements. Speak with your account manager for specifics.

Product & compliance

Formulation, certifications, and quality.

What types of gummies can you make?+

We are a contract manufacturer — effectively any gummy format our clients request. Pectin or gelatin base; vitamin, mineral, botanical, probiotic, melatonin, collagen, ACV, mushroom blend, amino acid, enzyme, prebiotic and fiber formulations. We build to your brief.

Can you make vegan or vegetarian gummies?+

Yes. Our pectin-based formulations are plant-derived, making them vegan and vegetarian friendly. We can also source certified vegan coatings and excipients for brands targeting that specific positioning.

Can you make sugar-free or low-sugar gummies?+

Yes. We formulate sugar-free, sugar-reduced, keto-friendly and low-glycemic gummies using alternative syrup bases (maltitol, sorbitol, xylitol, allulose) and natural high-intensity sweeteners (stevia, monk fruit).

Can you make allergen-free gummies?+

Absolutely. Our R&D team formulates gluten-free, soy-free, dairy-free and Top-9 allergen-free gummies. We will work with you to identify the right raw materials and confirm suitability for your target claims.

What active ingredients can you work with?+

Vitamins, minerals, amino acids, antioxidants, omega fatty acids, collagen and other proteins, probiotics, prebiotics, botanicals, herbal extracts, enzymes, fibers, adaptogens and functional mushroom blends. If there is a specific branded ingredient you need, we will source it.

What certifications does your facility hold?+

Gabe US is FDA Registered and operates under cGMP standards in compliance with 21 CFR 111 (dietary supplement manufacturing regulations). Every batch is manufactured in our Lorton, Virginia facility — made in the USA.

Do you provide a Certificate of Analysis (COA)?+

Yes. Every finished batch ships with a full Certificate of Analysis documenting active-ingredient potency, microbial testing, heavy metals, residual solvents and organoleptic evaluation. Your label claims hold up to retailer and FDA scrutiny.

Where is your facility located?+

Our manufacturing facility is at 7951 Angleton Ct Ste 1A, Lorton, Virginia 22079 — just outside Washington, D.C. Every gummy we make is produced here, never offshored and never sub-contracted.

How do you perform quality control?+

Multi-stage QC from inbound raw material verification through finished product. Every batch is tested in-house and by independent third-party labs for potency, microbials, heavy metals and residual solvents. We document everything — transparent procedures, independent audits, full traceability.

Do you help with label and regulatory compliance?+

Yes. Our regulatory team reviews Supplement Facts panels, structure/function claims, allergen declarations and 21 CFR 111 requirements. We help you avoid the common labeling mistakes that get products pulled from shelves or gated on Amazon.

Can you make gummies with my logo or a custom shape?+

Yes. We have over 100 existing mold shapes — bears, hearts, domes, cubes, stars, rings and more. For larger programs we can also tool custom molds featuring your logo or a brand-specific shape. Ask your account manager about custom tooling lead times and minimums.
