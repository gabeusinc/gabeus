The private-label workflow

Seven decisions, one fully customized gummy program.

01
Inquiry & Quote

Tell us your concept, target potency and volume. Standard formulas are quoted immediately; custom formulations in 24–48 hours.

02
Gummy Technology

Pectin-based (plant-derived, vegan-friendly) or gelatin-based (traditional bite, wider active-ingredient compatibility).

03
Formula Selection

Choose from our proven formulas, collaborate on a custom development, or bring your own existing formula for us to scale.

04
Flavor Profile

A wide range of natural and nature-identical flavor systems — fruit, citrus, berry, tropical, sour, herbal blends and more.

05
Color Selection

100+ color options — natural plant-based colors (beet, turmeric, spirulina) or certified synthetic systems for maximum vibrancy.

06
Shape & Coating

Over 100 mold designs — bears, hearts, domes, cubes, rings, stars, custom logos. Sugar-sanded or oil-coated for finish and shelf stability.

07
Packaging

Bottles, stand-up pouches, jars, sachets or bulk bags. Custom labels, induction seals, tamper-evident bands — retail-ready.
