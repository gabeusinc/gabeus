How it works

Seven stages, one account manager, zero hand-offs.

Our vertically integrated team compresses what usually takes nine months at legacy co-mans into a matter of weeks. Every project follows the same transparent path.

01
Inquiry & NDA

You share your concept. We counter-sign a mutual NDA so your formula and label IP are protected from day one.

02
Requirements

A discovery call locks in active-ingredient dosages, flavor direction, dietary spec and compliance requirements.

03
Formulation

Food chemists develop the gummy to your taste, texture and potency targets. Every ingredient is vetted against FDA regs.

04
Sample & Revise

You evaluate physical samples. We revise flavor, color, shape and potency until the sample matches your brief.

05
Quote & PO

Standard formulas quoted instantly. Custom within 24–48 hours. Full breakdown — no hidden fees.

06
Production

Pilot run validates the formula at scale. Manufacturing follows under cGMP with continuous QC testing.

07
Delivery

Labels applied, induction-sealed, tamper-evident banded. Full Certificate of Analysis with every shipment.
