Services

Full-service gummy manufacturing — under one roof.

From concept to labeled bottle: formulation, production, testing, packaging and regulatory support all happen inside our FDA-registered facility in Lorton, Virginia. No external labs, no sub-contractors, no hand-offs.

What we provide

Six in-house capabilities, one account manager.

🧪

Custom Formulation & R&D

Our in-house food chemists and nutritionists develop original gummy formulas to your exact spec — active ingredients, potency, taste, texture, color and shape. We work with vitamins, minerals, amino acids, antioxidants, omega fatty acids, proteins, probiotics, prebiotics, botanicals, herbs, enzymes and fibers. Every formulation is built for compliance and shelf stability.

🏷️

Private Label

Skip the R&D cycle and launch in weeks. Choose from our library of proven gummy formulas, then customize flavor, shape, color, coating and packaging to match your brand. Ideal for emerging brands, retailers adding a house line, and e-commerce operators testing new categories.

🏭

Contract Manufacturing

Bring us your existing formula and scale it in an FDA-registered, cGMP-compliant facility. Pectin (vegan) or gelatin-based. Sugar-reduced, sugar-free, keto, paleo, organic, non-GMO, allergen-free — whatever dietary spec your product requires, we build to it.

🔬

Quality & Compliance Testing

Every batch undergoes multi-stage in-house and independent third-party testing: potency verification, microbial, heavy metals, residual solvents and organoleptic evaluation. Full Certificate of Analysis delivered with every shipment so your label claims hold up to retailer and FDA scrutiny.

📦

Packaging & Fulfillment

Bottles, stand-up pouches, jars, sachets or bulk bags. In-line filling, induction sealing, tamper-evident banding, custom label application and retail-ready finishing. Over 100 mold shapes and coating options (sugar sanding or oil) to make your gummies stand out on the shelf.

📋

Regulatory & Label Support

Our regulatory team guides you through FDA dietary supplement requirements, 21 CFR 111 compliance, Supplement Facts panel structure, structure/function claims review and allergen labeling. We help you avoid the labeling mistakes that get products pulled from shelves or gated on Amazon.

How it works

Seven stages. One account manager. Zero hand-offs.

Our vertically integrated team compresses what usually takes nine months at legacy co-mans into a matter of weeks. Every project follows the same transparent path below.

01
Inquiry & NDA

You share your concept, target demographic, claims and volume estimate. We counter-sign a mutual NDA so your formula concepts and label IP are protected from day one.

02
Requirements & Scoping

A detailed discovery call with our formulation and regulatory team locks in active-ingredient dosages, flavor direction, dietary spec, target shelf life and compliance requirements.

03
Custom Formulation

Our in-house food chemists develop the gummy to your requested taste, texture, potency and appearance. Every ingredient is vetted against FDA dietary supplement regulations before it goes in the formula.

04
Sample & Revision

You evaluate physical samples. We revise flavor, texture, color, shape and potency as many times as it takes until you sign off. Only then do we move to production planning.

05
Quote & Purchase Order

Standard formulas receive immediate pricing. Fully custom formulations are quoted within 48 hours. PO is placed with a standard 50% deposit; full balance due before raw material procurement begins.

06
Plant Trial & Production

A pilot run at scale validates the formula on production equipment before the full batch. Manufacturing follows under cGMP controls with continuous QC testing: potency, microbials, heavy metals, residual solvents.

07
Packaging & Delivery

Bottled, pouched, or bulk — your choice. Custom labels applied, induction sealed, tamper-evident banded. Full Certificate of Analysis included with every shipment.
